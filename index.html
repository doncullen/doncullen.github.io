About Myself

I've never been much for writing my own bio, so I'll just toss in here what's on the top of my head. I'm an experienced Software Engineer and Web Developer. I have a Bachelor's degree in Business Administration with a focus on marketing and management. I currently live in Austin, Texas. Well — Buda, Texas — actually. But hey, it still counts. I'm a proud dad of two awesome kids and am happily married to a supermom. My areas of passion (other than the kids) include investing, philosophy, deaf community, software engineering, and technological innovations. Seriously, I geek out when it's Tesla, SpaceX, Hyperloop, Alcor, and all of the others! I also immensely enjoy reading books and am an avid researcher. That is, if you call Googling the hell out of something "research". For those who want a peek into how I see life; I'm a singularitarian, transhuman, and an extropian. I’m also an agnostic theist. Oh, and I'm also a blogger who writes about science, tech, medicine, politics, law, and deaf-related issues.
